Why it matters

Meet & greets keep pups confident and routines seamless.

Think of it as a stress-free dress rehearsal. We learn your cues, your dog's energy, and the quirks that make home feel safe so day-one walks feel like reunions instead of new introductions.

Trust building

Dogs relax faster when they've already exchanged sniffs and treats with their walker.

Home walkthrough

Show us leashes, harnesses, treat drawers, and alarm panels so we can replicate your routine.

Care planning

We note meds, feeding cues, reactivity triggers, and preferred walking routes.

Concierge touches

Customize updates, post-walk rituals, and access preferences before the first booking.
